Fun Facts about cats

  • Whisker Sensitivity: A cat's whiskers are super sensitive and help them navigate their surroundings. They can even detect tiny changes in the air, helping them sense nearby objects or movement in the dark.
  • Cats Can Rotate Their Ears: A cat’s ears can rotate 180 degrees! They have 32 muscles in each ear, allowing them to be highly directional when listening.
  • Kneading Behavior: Cats knead with their paws, often on soft surfaces like blankets or their owner's lap. This behavior stems from kittenhood, where they kneaded their mother’s belly to stimulate milk production.
